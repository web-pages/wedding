THE AFTER PARTY - SAN FERMINES!

- The Running Of The Bulls -

- Pamplona Town Hall -

After the wedding, don't be too quick to leave! Pamplona (which is only a 45 minute drive from San Sebastian) has it's annual San Fermines party on Jul 6th. One of the biggest parties in the world, San Fermines is probably best known by one of it's traditions: The running of the bulls.

San Fermines starts on 6 July. Joining in the party requires one to follow the dress code: White pants and t-shirt along with a red sash (called a pañuelico) - seriously, even dogs are dressed up. These can be obtained (quite cheaply) in Pamplona

We will be around until at least after the first running of the bulls (on the 7th of July) and we hope to see as many of you as possible. Pamplona is a unqiue city with an amazing gastronmical culture. Best yet, Itzi and her friends know all the best places to eat and party!
